Resources Used

fIGMA

Figma is a web-based graphics editing and user interface design app. You can use it to do all kinds of graphic design work from wireframing websites, designing mobile app interfaces, prototyping designs, crafting social media posts, and everything in between. Figma is different from other graphics editing tools. Mainly because it works directly on your browser. This means you get to access your projects and start designing from any computer or platform without having to buy multiple licenses or install software. Another reason why designers love this app is that Figma offers a generous free plan where you can create and store 3 active projects at a time. It's more than enough for you to learn, experiment, and work on small projects.

FRAMER

Framer is a powerful design and prototyping tool that allows you to create interactive user interfaces (UIs) for web and mobile applications. It provides an intuitive interface where you can visually design components, animations, and interactions. Framer also supports code-based customization, making it a favorite among designers and developers. Whether you're creating a simple button or a complex UI flow, Framer empowers you to bring your ideas to life!

Framer form :

A Framer Form is a powerful feature that allows you to collect information from website visitors without writing any code. With Framer Forms, you can design and customize your forms, capturing data like email signups or client inquiries. You get over 10 unique input types, freeform editing, and options to send submissions via email, Google Sheets, or custom webhooks. Plus, it's secure with spam protection and rate limiting
